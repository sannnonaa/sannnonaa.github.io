Bangkirai Hill

This tour offers the charm of an unspoiled tropical rainforest, which is equipped with tourist facilities and infrastructure such as restaurants, lamin for meetings, swimming pools, and cottages and jungle cabins. In this area there is a 64 m long canopy bridge that hangs connecting 5 Bangkirai trees at a height of 30 m. This canopy bridge is the first in Indonesia, the second in the country, and the second in the world. This canopy bridge is the first in Indonesia, the second in Asia and the eighth in the world.
